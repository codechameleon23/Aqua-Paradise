Chimney Repairs in Salisbury

The chimney stack is a weak point in any roof and can be the primary cause if you are experiencing dampness on your chimney breast.

If chimneys are left un-maintained given time they can deteriorate. This can lead to parts of them dislodging and dropping and causing damage to the roof and in the worst cases collapsing

From simple re-flashing of lead to a complete over hall of your chimney stack we can provide all these services.

We also offer a re-pointing service. This involves the re-application of mortar to the joints between bricks, which may have weathered away over the years. In very extreme cases the flaunching on the top of the chimney can crack and dislodge the chimney pots leading to the chimney pots falling off the roof often causing damage.
