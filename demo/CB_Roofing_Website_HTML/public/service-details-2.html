Velux Roof Windows by CB Roofing Salisbury

Looking for a Velux roof window fiitter in Salisbury, Wiltshire?

Velux produce a huge range of windows and skylights and have products for both pitched and flat roofs. In addition they also offer a range of roller blinds, remote controls and sun screening.

From simple re-flashing of lead to a complete over hall of your chimney stack we can provide all these services.

We also offer a re-pointing service. This involves the re-application of mortar to the joints between bricks, which may have weathered away over the years. In very extreme cases the flaunching on the top of the chimney can crack and dislodge the chimney pots leading to the chimney pots falling off the roof often causing damage.

Safety

Current building regulations mean that each converted loft must meet access and emergencyrequirements; this often requires the installation of an escape window. Velux produce special escape windows that can be installed as roof lights.

Flat roof windows

Because Velux windows are normally seen on pitch roofs, it is often assume that they are unsuitable for flat roof installation but this is not the case. There are range of flat roof and shallow pitch windows that can create a great look and increase natural daylight into the room. Velux also produce a range of blinds to fit all of their range of windows.

We are skilled in the installation of velux windows and can guarantee a great finish.

Benefits of Velux Windows

  • Can help create a usable space
  • Provides natural daylight
  • Provides ventilation
  • Future-proofs your roof space
  • Double glazing - keeps heat in and noise out
  • Toughened glass for security
  • Adjustable friction hinges
  • Central pivot - for cleaning external glazing
