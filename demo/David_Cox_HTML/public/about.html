Mr David Cox

Consultant Orthospanaedic Surgeon

Mr David Cox is a renowned Orthopaedic Surgeon, specialising in knee and hip surgery and the treatment of sports injuries. He runs a busy orthopaedic practice undertaking a high volume of complex knee and hip replacements each year. Concentrating in minimal access and keyhole procedures, he utilizes the most modern techniques to aid a fast recovery from surgery.

Education & Training

Mr David Cox qualified in medicine from Charing Cross Hospital Medical School in 1981, teaching at Cambridge University, his subsequent postgraduate surgical training was at St Bartholomews Hospital London as a Registrar, and St Thomasâ€™ Hospital London as a Senior Registrar.

He undertook further training in knee surgery in Boston USA in 1995, and was appointed to Salisbury Foundation Trust as a trauma and orthopaedic consultant in 1996.
