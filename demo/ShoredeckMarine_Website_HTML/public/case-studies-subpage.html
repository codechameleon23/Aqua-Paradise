Services | Limehouse Marina

Limehouse Marina

Client: BWML

Objective

To replace existing wooden deck boards which had severe wood rot and posed danger to marina users. Relocate the service pontoons and stabilise. Improve stability of main walkway pontoons and reduce existing listing.

Criteria

New decking had to be durable and relatively maintenance free whilst maintaining an anti-slip surface.

Method

Removal of existing deck boards and disposal. Installation of new GRP MiniMesh panels. Disconnected and moved services pontoon and used galvanised steel underslung beams to stabilise to adjacent pontoons. Distributed concrete weights to improve listing of main walkway.
