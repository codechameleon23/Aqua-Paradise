Decking Options

Hardwood

These can all be supplied full length, cut to size or have grip deck inserts for added nonslip. Can supply only or supply and install or just install.

We also supply fender and bearer sections in these materials.

Other options include deckboard treatment including staining and oiling.

Composite

Composite timber is an ideal product for pontoon deck boards due to its low maintenance and barefoot friendly surface. Not only is it water resistant, rot free and UV stable, it is environmentally friendly and contains up to 87% recycled material.

The boards are available in several different widths and have several different colour options including Teak, Charcoal, Oak and Ash.

GRP Grating

GRP fibreglass grating is ideal for areas that need just a little extra durability against harsher environments. Utilising its nonslip top surface, the grating is perfect for ramps and slipways, fuel berths, bridges & much more.

The GRP grating is available in different thicknesses, has open or solid top options and has a range of colours as stock items.
