Contact Us

The Department of Sexual Health at Salisbury District Hospital has appointment and walk-in clinics. Our walk-in clinics allow patients to attend without an appointment and patients are seen in order of arrival.

The staff here specialise in:

  • Screening and treating Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs)
  • Contraception of all kinds (including all emergency contraception)
  • HIV care (including Post Exposure Prophylaxis)
  • Genital skin conditions
  • Pregnancy testing
  • Cervical screening (smears)
  • Condoms
