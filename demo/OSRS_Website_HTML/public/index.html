What is resin bound stone?

Resin bound stone is porous unique paving system which consists of kiln dried natural aggregate combined with a UV stable aliphatic 2 part polyurethane resin.

Resin bound stone has many advantages such as it is extremely hard wearing, suds compliant, porous, water does not puddle, anti slip, easy to maintain and extremely attractive. If you wish for a natural gravel look with out all those loose stones combined with a beautiful seamless finish then resin bound stone is the answer. Whether it’s a contemporary or a rustic look you want to achieve then this is all possible with the right colour choice.

We also sell all the profiles so you can offer patterns, shapes or even logos into your design.
