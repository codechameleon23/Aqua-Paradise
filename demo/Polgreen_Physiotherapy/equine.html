What are the benefits of Equine Physiotherapy?

Horses are considered the most common species of animal to receive physiotherapy. The purpose is to restore mobility and performance and maintain it. Equine physiotherapy has many applications for horses. Like people horse can compensate for pain by moving in a different manner, meaning conditions can go unnoticed for extended periods of time. By concentrating on the affected area we can make a detailed assessment and use physiotherapy to treat specific areas.

Physiotherapy uses a range of treatment tools in conjunction with exercise therapy. We believe that this is the best way to gain the most from your animal. Physiotherapy post injury and on going throughout an animals life can have several benefits.

Physiotherapy Benefits

  • Improved Performance
  • Optimised Healing Times
  • Improved Strength, Movement and Flexibility
  • Pain Relief
  • Improved Quality of Life
  • Joint and Soft Tissue Care

Initial Process and Assessment

Understanding the Injury and Assessment

At this point we will have a better understanding of the injury or problems and be able to discuss with you the way forward. We will always discuss the findings with you to ensure you have a full understanding of your horses problems and help to answer any questions you may have. During the initially assessment there is usually time to start the treatment and rehabilitation programme and your physiotherapist may give you some exercises to start.
