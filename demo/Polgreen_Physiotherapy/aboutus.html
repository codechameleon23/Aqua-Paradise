Megan Merrifield-Jones

Megan started her career on leaving school and becoming a working pupil for a year at Huntley School of Equitation near Gloucester completing her BHS exams to become a BHSAI.

Working with show horses and then eventers for a couple of years she saw horses have physiotherapy and decided to go back into education.

Completing her A levels and qualifying as a Chartered Physiotherapist from The University of Huddersfield in 2011, gaining her BSc (hons) degree, Megan moved to Wiltshire to work in the NHS specialising in Musculoskeletal Outpatients.

During this time she started her Masters in Veterinary Physiotherapy at Hartpury University Centre part time. On qualifying she now solely focuses on her business working full time privately.

In her spare time she likes long distance running and has two horses of her own which she competes when she can.
