WALK DORSET

The grove is perfectly situated as a hub to enable our
visitors to explore the beautiful landscapes of dorset.

The Grove is perfectly situated on the A30 between Shaftesbury and Salisbury with good commuter links by car from the A303, A30 and A350.

In relation to travelling by train The Grove is served well by both Tisbury Railway Station which is 3 miles away and Gillingham which is 10 miles from the Grove, both have taxi’s to deliver you to the door and are mainline London Waterloo stations.

You can also arrive by helicopter/plane to the Grove – with Compton Abbas Airfield directly above the village of Ludwell, the perfect way to make your arrival or simply worth exploring during your stay!
