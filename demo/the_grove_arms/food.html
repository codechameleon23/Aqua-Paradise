SUE CRAIG

Sue heads our kitchen team with passion and pride, producing the best home cooked food for miles.

Sue moved to Melbury Abbas, Shaftesbury in the 90’s whilst raising 3 beautiful children. As Sue’s children grew older it was time for Sue to pave a career for herself and turn a passion for home cooking into an income to support the growing brood.

Sue decided to return to work at a local tearooms, it started as a small project but, with their great management and Sue’s talent for home cooking, it wasn’t long before it turned into a more lucrative venture. After four great years Sue felt she needed a new challenge, so her next step was to move to Port Regis to assist as part of the kitchen team producing fantastic food including lunches, functions and afternoon teas when required. A far busier role that inspired Sue and helped her think more about where her cooking could take her.

Unfortunately the unthinkable was just around the corner for Sue and her much loved career was once again put on hold, as illness meant she had to stop work to look after her Mum. Sue’s Mum had taught her all the cookery skills she uses today, including the ability to cook a phenomenal pie and produce gravy spoken about for miles around.

This food, along with our atmosphere allows all who visit The Grove the chance to make special memories with loved ones around the table - this is where Sue’s passion began and is at the heart of every dish she serves.

SUE AT THE GROVE

Sue joined the family at The Grove in February 2016 - and for the first time Sue was able to achieve success in her own right doing what she loves best, cooking her home cooked food.

17 months on Sue is driven by the positive feedback we receive daily on her creations, and how she takes the most simple dishes but executes them with love creating flavoursome food for all.

The menu at The Grove is based on great produce, using local produce sourced fresh every day, made into simple yet delicious home cooked favourites seen in the main menu and on the specials board. Sue will be with us for many years - so why not join the family for a food experience to remember!
