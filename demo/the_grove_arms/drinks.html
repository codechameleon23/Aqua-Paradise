OUR DRINKS

NINA IS A BUSINESS PARTNER WITH HALL & WOODHOUSE.

The Grove stocks Hall & Woodhouse cask conditioned ales, refreshing lagers and ciders, and wholesome stouts – all kept at the perfect temperature. As well as our wonderful cask on pump, we also stock the full range of Hall & Woodhouse’s delicious bottled beers.

The Grove isn’t all about ale, it also have a wide gin selection, a wine selection to celebrate any occasion and a soft drinks offering to support any family day out.

Cocktails are also available at the Grove throughout the summer season and mulled wine is a seasonal edition to warn off the winter blues – there is nothing like the sweet smell of mulled wine at Christmas time!
