How to get to odstock medical

By Car

Salisbury is at the crossing point of the London to Exeter line and the Bristol to Southampton and Brighton line. Trains from London leave from Waterloo station. Travelling from the Midlands or North, change at either Bristol or Basingstoke. For train enquiries phone 0044 (0)345 48 49 50 or check http://www.nationalrail.co.uk/

The hospital is a 3km taxi ride from the station. Busses run every 10 minutes at peak times and less often at other times. For more information on local buses phone Salisbury Reds on 0845 072 7093 or check their web page.
