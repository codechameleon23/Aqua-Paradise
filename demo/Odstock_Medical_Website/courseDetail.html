One-day courses are aimed at clinicians who may be seeing a small number of FES patients and/or using their FES skills to assess patients and refer on to an FES centre for full treatment.

This course concentrates on the theory of FES and the practical application (see details and learning outcomes one day course for more information). It is an ideal course also for anyone who would like to learn more about FES and its clinical applications in gait assist.

Cost: £229 excl. VAT.
