Neurophysiotherapy

  • Home
  • Treatments
  • Neurophysiotherapy

At OML, we deliver a specialist rehabilitation service for individuals with neurological conditions.

Conditions that commonly benefit from physiotherapy:

  • Stroke
  • Parkinson’s Disease
  • Multiple Sclerosis
  • Spinal Cord Injury
  • Cerebral Palsy
  • Traumatic Brain Injury

We strive to utilise the latest evidence base to inform our practice.

WHAT CAN WE OFFER?

  • When you attend neurophysiotherapy, you will receive an individual assessment together with your clinician, treatment can be planned.
  • Everyone is different and conditions can vary, so we design each programme to your needs.
  • During this assessment, we will talk through the treatment and the number of sessions you will need.
  • Working as an individual or in a group, can be part of your programme.

There are many benefits to doing this:

  • As an individual, you can benefit from a personalised approach; and
  • In a group, you work with others who may be experiencing similar difficulties.

Individual or group exercise can benefit from functional activities such as walking and daily tasks which can enhance your general wellbeing.
