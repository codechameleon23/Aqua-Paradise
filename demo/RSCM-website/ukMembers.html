RSCM UK Memberships

The RSCM offers a wide range of benefits to members, to help you and the other musicians in your worshipping community make the best music possible. It is also possible to renew your membership quickly and efficiently using our online renewal facility (you must have a website login in order to use this facility) https://www.rscm.com/renew-your-membership/

For a helpful simple guide to renewing your membership online please see our Guidelines for renewing your membership online. If you are already a current RSCM member, we would love you to take two minutes to complete our short 2 minute survey which will help us develop and tailor our current resources as well as helping us to introduce news ones in the future.

For further information on RSCM Memberships and benefits in the UK (including Northern Ireland), the Channel Islands and the Isle of Man; please see below. For ways in which you can support the RSCM, even if you are not active in church music, please click here. We are very grateful for every donation, of any size, which goes towards the RSCM’s work in supporting and training church musicians.

Affiliate Memberships

Churches, schools and community choirs are invited to affiliate to the RSCM. This type of membership is for organisations who wish to play an active part in our work.

An Affiliate membership provides the following benefits:

  • Free subscription to two copies of Church Music Quarterly every quarter, the magazine dedicated to church musicians, offering a mix of news, features, recommendations and reviews, and the latest news from the RSCM (also available in digital format from www.rscmshop.com for members)
  • Free subscription to two copies every quarter of Sunday by Sunday, the RSCM Worship Planner, which gives week-by-week guidance on choosing music and liturgy throughout the year (also available in digital format from www.rscmshop.com for members)
  • Recommended fees for church musicians – updated annually (available online from RSCM Music Direct for Members Only).
  • Discount on classified adverts in Church Music Quarterly
  • The Network – information on locally organised events and courses (many are discounted to RSCM members)
  • Free subscription to the RSCM email newsletter, with news of the RSCM and the world of church music (optional)
  • 25% discount on copies of RSCM publications bought through RSCM Music Direct
  • Discount for places on all nationally organised RSCM courses
  • The eligibility to enter singers for RSCM Voice For Life awards
  • Discount on choir robes from J Wippell & Co, and Watts & Company
  • Guidance and support from the RSCM Office

Large parishes, teams or benefices may purchase extra copies of the quarterly magazines by applying for, or upgrading to, an “Affiliate Plus” membership.

*see Discount Offer criteria above. Terms and conditions apply.

Fees are current until the end of December each year, and are renewable annually. We operate four renewal periods:
January – December | April – March | July – June | October – September

When you apply to join you will be added into the nearest period.
