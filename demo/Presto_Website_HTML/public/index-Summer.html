By signing up to Presto each week you will receive a day by day meal plan in a downloadable format. This will include a shopping list and the step by step recipes needed to create each meal.

At the start of each week we will email you with that weeks shopping list and recipe guide. The shopping list will come to no more than £20 and will be readily available ingredients easily found in most major supermarkets. All you need to do is go out, purchase the ingredients and get cooking!

Previous meals have included:

  • Preheat the oven to 180C and boil a kettle.
  • Pour the water from the kettle into a pot, set the pot on the hob and turn the heat up to medium. Add a pinch of salt to the water.
  • Once boiling again, put the potato chunks into the water.
  • Heat the olive oil in a frying pan over a medium heat, once hot add the onions and leave to caramelise, stirring occasionally. You want them to get all caramelised and sticky, so don’t rush them. Season with salt and pepper.
  • Put the sausages in the oven, they should take about 15 minutes.
  • When the potatoes are soft enough that you can easily push through them with a knife, take them off the heat and use a colander to drain them.
  • Put the butter and sour cream in with the potatoes and start mashing them up. You can use a fork or potato masher, it doesn’t matter, but you want a smooth creamy consistency. Season with salt and pepper to your liking.
  • Once the onions are soft and sticky, take them off the heat and set aside.
  • The sausages will be done when they’re cooked all the way through. If you’re unsure then cutting one open and having a look will tell you. You shouldn’t see any pink in the centre of the sausage, if you do stick it back in the oven for 5 minutes.
