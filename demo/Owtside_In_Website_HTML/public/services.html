Our accompanying services

Buying beautiful objects is one thing but considering their setting can also be vital An experienced eye and familiarity with spatial design can be a great help in assessing the possibilities of a garden and house, Owtside + In

We are unique in providing services of Advice, Sourcing and Bespoke Design

Advice...

Identifying your ideal balance of uses for outdoor and indoor spaces is often a first step to finding the right objects to fill or accent them.

We help you decide what you want to achieve and suggest styling solutions to bring these areas to life.

We start with a phone conversation and see where it leads us – usually to reviewing a video together of the areas in question or a visit to explore the possibilities.

Sourcing...

Quite possibly we already have items in our Stock Room that would be a perfect fit but we also have the imagination and contact base to present you with new ideas and alternatives.

We can scour archives and sources to find the exceptional and perfect solution.

Bespoke
design...

Our other contribution is to design the layout and content of your outside space in a way that interacts seamlessly with the inside of the house

We can work as principals or alongside your garden designers, architects and interior designers to ensure these transitional spaces flow together

A further part of the solution can be our design of bespoke furniture and objects that cross over the boundaries - Owtside + In
