Signing-up For Gift Aid

If you are a UK taxpayer, you can increase the value of your donations to SSIT at no extra cost to yourself by signing up to the Gift Aid scheme. This allows SSIT to reclaim tax from HMRC. You can make your Gift Aid declaration on line quickly and easily. Please follow the link below. (If you would rather use a paper form and post it to us, you can print the Donation Form at the foot of this page instead).

When to Get in Touch

Please notify SSIT if you:

  • Change your name or home address
  • No longer pay sufficient tax on your income and/or capital gains.

Higher Rate Tax Payers

If you pay income tax at the higher rate, you must include all your Gift Aid donations on your Self Assessment tax return if you want to receive the additional tax relief due to you.
