Sell Your Property With Us

Evans & Partridge has been situated in the attractive Test Valley town of Stockbridge since 1991 and for all this time has concentrated on the sale of middle to high end individual properties specialising in the country and villages, including equestrian holdings, in an extensive area, mainly from north of Romsey, to the south of Newbury and west to Salisbury and beyond.

This specialisation makes us acknowledged experts in the field. Whether looking to buy or sell, contact us for the best advice and a service, second to none.

The Guild Of Professional Estate Agents

Evans & Partridge are members of The Guild of Property Professionals, a national network of approximately 800 successful independent agents, all selected for their quality of service, that deal with approximately 65,000 properties annually.

Their monthly magazine is available from all participating agencies across the country.

Guild members are able to provide access to the national and international market through The Guild’s showroom in London’s Park Lane.
