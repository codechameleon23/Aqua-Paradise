Houghton, Stockbridge, Hampshire SO20

A unique property designed to take full advantage of the privileged elevated position and a magnificent open vista over the River Test and uninterrupted countryside beyond, quietly situated on the edge of this popular village, just a short drive from Stockbridge High Street.

DESCRIPTION

A unique detached house designed and built for the present owners in 2008/9 constructed of brick and cedar clad elevations beneath a tiled roof. The main section of the house features a fabulous oak frame by ‘Oakwrights’ with vaulted ceiling and extensive glazed areas providing light and airy open plan living. All of the principal rooms are well placed to enjoy the stunning position and views over the River Test. It is worth noting that the surrounding countryside is a Site of Specific Scientific Interest, the house and adjoining paddock (not part of Drayton Corner) is situated outside the village settlement boundary and there is minimal light pollution.

Features include: ‘Ice Energy’ ground source heat pump with underfloor heating throughout the majority of the property; rainwater harvesting for garden use; beautifully fitted kitchen with stainless steel French range, Aga and polished lava work surfaces; stylish bathrooms and wet room; engineered oak flooring and oak skirting boards with solid oak panelled internal doors; ‘SmartVac’ central vacuum system and ‘Clipsal’ Smart Home Technology with Cat5 wiring throughout.

LOCATION

The property is situated on the edge of the highly sought after village of Houghton, which has delightful walks traversing the River Test and on to the renowned Test Way. There is also an excellent public house/restaurant (The Boot Inn) and a church, both within a short stroll. Further facilities can be found in the neighbouring town of Stockbridge (two miles) which offers a variety of shops, a Post Office, hotels, restaurants and public houses, churches and a doctor’s surgery. The cathedral cities of Salisbury and Winchester are both within a twenty minute drive, and the A303 is close at hand allowing convenient access to London and the West Country.

SCHOOLING AND RECREATION There is excellent schooling (private and state) in the area. There are junior schools in Nether Wallop and Broughton (rated ‘good’) as well as Stockbridge which also has as secondary school; Kings and Peter Symonds Sixth Form College are located in Winchester and there are Grammar Schools in Salisbury. Farleigh Preparatory School is within a short drive; other local public schools include Winchester College and St Swithun’s in Winchester, Godolphin, Chaffyn Grove and the Cathedral School in Salisbury. There is excellent fishing on the River Test and a golf course in Leckford as well as two in Andover.
