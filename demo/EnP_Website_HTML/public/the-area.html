The Beautiful Test Valley

Evan & Partridge are fortunate in having a superb well established and centrally situated office on Stockbridge High Street, in the heart of the beautiful Test Valley.

We are surrounded by attractive villages and hamlets often divided by a crystal clear chalk stream or winterbourne flowing on its way to join the River Test. This famous river, said by some to be the centre of the best known trout fishing in the world, passes through the popular town of Stockbridge, the base of the very select Houghton Fishing Club, which owns a considerable stretch of fishing rights.

Our Area of Operation

As a company we offer properties for sale from the southern edge of Newbury to the edge of the Abbey town of Romsey, towards the Cathedral cities of Winchester to the east and Salisbury to the west, and beyond.

One of the many strengths of the area is the excellent communications, with mainline rail services to London (Waterloo) in about an hour from Andover, with other stations in Grateley, Whitchurch and Winchester. The A303 from the West Country to London (via the M3 motorway) can be accessed just to the south of Andover, while to the east of the town, the A34 road to Oxford also joins the M4 to the north of Newbury.

Easy Reach of London

Those who work in Winchester, Salisbury, Newbury or Basingstoke can appreciate living in the Test Valley and enjoy an easy daily commute through beautiful countryside. There is an excellent selection of schooling in the area, from highly regarded primary schools in the villages to reputable secondary schools and sixth form colleges in the towns as well as good choice of private schools.

With the area’s stunning countryside, good communications and picturesque villages with their varied accommodation and character, it is only to be expected that there is a keen demand for houses in the area within such easy reach of London.
