TRAIN

Salisbury train station is located at the south western edge of the city and just a 10 minute walk from the Christmas Market.

Salisbury is on the main London and Bristol railway lines and has direct links to Southampton, Bath, Basingstoke and Exeter.

Groups

Salisbury Christmas Market is a great place to come with a group, with many coach companies running day tours.

If arriving by coach, our Coach Ambassadors will be on hand to meet and greet visitors with Christmas in Salisbury guides and advice on directions, places to eat, what’s on and more.
