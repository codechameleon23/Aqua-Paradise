Speculative Work

The Presence of Christ

Total Cost £2,600

Sarum College Exhibition “The Word”
Portland relief carving 22” x 25” x 2” painted acrylic and gilded.

This carving is in a style that was prevalent when the KJB was first printed but shows the changing landscape over the last 4 centuries. It is painted and gilded using as bright colours as possible, as was most stone carving of the day.

A view of a direct relationship with Christ informed by The King James Bible over the last 400 years, colouring and illuminating the World.
