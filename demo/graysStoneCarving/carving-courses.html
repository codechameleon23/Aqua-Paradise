LEARN TO CARVE IN A SUPPORTIVE AND ENCOURAGING ENVIRONMENT

The courses are suitable for people with varying carving skills - from the complete beginner to the more experienced stone carver. You will be guided through the whole process by an experienced tutor, taught good working practice, tool handling and the basic stone carving techniques.

Students can work on their own designs or the tutor can suggest a start point for a variety of projects ranging from sculpture in the round, relief carving, or architectural carving, creating your own unique piece of work to take home at the end of the course.
