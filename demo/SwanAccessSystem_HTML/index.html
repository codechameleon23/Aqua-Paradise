Who we are

Swan Access Systems supply, install, maintain and repair a wide range of electric gates, automated barriers and rising bollards across the south. We pride ourselves on delivering high quality installations that will give reliability and style at the entrance to your home or business.

From the initial free site survey we will talk through your requirements and work with you to make sure you get an installation tailored to you and the environment in which it will be installed.

Our fixed price installations are extremely competitive and unlike other companies we do not book our installers on back to back installations. This means we are not working to a deadline on your installation and nothing is rushed. The benefit is much more reliable, quality installations by happy installers.

Setting the standard

All of our projects apply rigorous standards in safety, workmanship and customer service. Here are just some of the things you should expect from us:

  • All jobs are risk assessed. As we are DHF (powered gate group trained) all installations are safe, compliant and CE marked.
  • NICEIC approved contractor.
  • Any new electrical supply to the gate is installed by fully qualified electricians. We can even provide any electrical work in the premises should this be required.
  • We offer warranties on all our installations & included annual maintenance.
  • Already have gates in place and need repair or on going maintenance we are always happy to help. We will provide you with a full condition report as well as a quote for any repair work/ safety upgrade work should it be required.
