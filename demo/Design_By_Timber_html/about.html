ABOUT US

Tim Hitchens, the very talented young designer behind Design by Timber has been designing furniture for the past fourteen years. Tim works in wood or metal creating bespoke pieces with a contemporary feel. A selection of beautiful and practical designs for the home or workplace are made to order, each with an identifiable and unique style. Products are modern, clean and multifunctional creating a focal point to any interior.

Tim is a pleasure to deal with and approaches all new projects with great enthusiasm. If you are visiting this website looking for ideas for a commercial project or a stunning custom made piece of furniture you will not be disappointed.
