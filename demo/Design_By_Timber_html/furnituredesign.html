FURNITURE DESIGN

Designing beautiful, bespoke furniture is how Design by Timber began and where Tim’s passion lies. Tim works in wood or metal creating bespoke pieces with a contemporary feel. A selection of beautiful and practical designs for the home or work place are made to order, each with an identifiable and unique style. Products are modern, clean and multifunctional creating a focal point to any interior.
