Guy hockley of
OWTSIDE + IN

Guy is an object maker, designer of spaces and trader in artefacts who has lived in London, New York and Tokyo and nowadays in an ancient hamlet in rural Wiltshire in walking distance of Stonehenge and Salisbury Cathedral.

As a countryman at heart who is also at home in capital cities, he has long been fascinated by the juxtapositions of cultures, eras, lifestyles and environments and the quest to fuse beauty and functionality in our surroundings.

His conviction that in life, art and nature that the best results come from balancing contrasts and combining opposites feeds the philosophy behind Owtside + In :

curating practical objects of the past to create beautiful environments, homes and gardens in today’s world
