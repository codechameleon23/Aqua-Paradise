What’s Been Happening, March 2017
07 / 04 / 2017

Back to News

It was one of those March days when the sun shines hot and the wind blows cold. When it is summer in the light and winter in the shade. CHARLES DICKENS

March…the beginning of Spring, the weather starts to warm,the flowers start to bloom, the evenings get lighter and last longer, it will be summer before we know it!

March just flew by for us here at Prolight, but we seemed to cram a lot in though. We packed some of the team off to Euroshop at the beginning of the month which apparently proved to be the largest Euroshop in its 50 year history!

We ran an advert in the A1 Retail Magazine alongside one of our customers Notcutts Garden Centre. Bit of a thing for us as its something we wouldn’t normally do but we are proud of who we are and the services we offer so why not shout about it every once in while!

Something else that shouts out is our new van signage, we think it looks great! We certainly can “brighten up your brand” – check out the photos. We’ve had cakes, visits from our Jezzmie’s four legged friend “Ted”. Russ and Jon have been tested on their DIY skills, we have even had time to send out some lighting for our customers!

JD Sports in Augsburg Germany, Zoetermeer Netherlands and Pamplona La Morea in Spain received their lighting kit this month and going further afield was River Island Bahrain, there is no limit to where we can supply you know!

River Island took their first delivery for the new Dudley Merryhill store, we are also on phase 2 of the Liverpool Church Street’s complete refit. Smiggle were busy this month opening new stores in Wimbledon and Eastbourne and preparing for Chester and Putney. We have also stayed local and helped out the Wiltshire Museum with some lighting too!
