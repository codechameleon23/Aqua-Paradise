We collaborated with Smiggle on thier brand new, 2 floored Oxford Street store.

Smiggle creates colourful, fun, fashion-forward stationary. Their goal is to inspire and develop the creative spirits of its customers. Consider it a sweet shop for stationary, if you can imagine it, you can probably find it.

By delivering original, fun and affordable stationery. Smiggle needed their lights to simply do the same and show off everything the store has to offer. That being said, it still needed to follow that creative outlook that they expect from their stores.

After working on 200+ stores with the Australian brand. Prolight has a strong relationship with Smiggle and have supplied the lighting for every UK store for the past 4 years.

This time around we took on the challenge that is its new Oxford street store. Prolight Designed, supplied and commissioned LED lighting scheme. As well as checking positioning was correct and efficient.
