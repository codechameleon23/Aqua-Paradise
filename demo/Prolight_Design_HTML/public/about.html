Who We Are

From shed to your favourite high-street shop: our lights, fittings and designs can be found in the likes of Burberry, Schuh, Lush, JD Sports, KFC to name just a few. Quite a trip from a backyard in Swindon to a £5million turnover last year. Every positive step along the way has been made possible by our passion for creating the best lighting solutions for every single one of our clients, and the hard-work and dedication of our incredible team.

We’re a friendly bunch so if you have any questions, don’t hesitate to get in touch. And why not come and meet us too?

Chiara Gill
manufacture

Project Co-ordinator

Chiara joined us in the workshop after graduating from Birmingham City Uni with a First in BA (Hons) Fine Art. She quickly moved into Project Administration where she’s crucial, and helps run some major projects. This involves a lot of organising and keeping an eye on the elements associated with this, and all done with an artistic flair of course.

Aside from being an imaginative and very talented artist, Chiara likes nothing more than crafting and seeking out a good bargain. She is also very creative in the kitchen.
