Our strategic Open House Events provide a particularly effective marketing approach with a 90% success rate and many homes featured having achieved well in excess of the asking price.

This is a method which can suit buyers with freedom to look and convenience for sellers. Our success rate in this field is very impressive with over 90% of our Open House Events leading to a satisfactory sale, many of which achieve significantly in excess of the asking price.
