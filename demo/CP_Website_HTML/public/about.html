About Charles Powell

Charles Powell is a unique and bespoke brand dedicated to the most personal client attention and synonymous with innovative and distinctive marketing.

Born as a family run business, we have enjoyed exceptional growth to become one of the local market leaders focussed on three important principles:

  • The dedication and diligence of our people in providing outstanding levels of service through the highest levels of communication and hard work.
  • The investment in sophisticated and intelligent marketing tailored to each individual home. From country manors to first time homes, we strive to provide the coverage and presentation deserving of your home.
  • The provision of value for money service by offering a dedicated service with a competitive fee rate.

Louis Powtell
Managing director

Louis Powell began his agency career in 1987 and enjoyed a career progression to become the youngest Managing Director of a 24 branch network of offices for the largest estate agency chain in the UK.

Keen to develop a more bespoke and discerning personal service, Charles Powell is his opportunity to provide a template for the modern boutique Estate Agency.

Louis lives with his wife Samantha and son Joe in the New Forest.
