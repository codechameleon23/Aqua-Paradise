Understanding
the Selling Process

At Charles Powell we are proud of our reputation as a communicative and helpful estate agent. We understand the need for regular updates and good communication whether through our 24 hour feedback following a viewing, progress on our latest marketing strategy or keeping abreast of the latest news on your sale and chain below.

We understand that selling a property can be confusing for some so will advise and guide you through the key milestones of the selling process at all times.
