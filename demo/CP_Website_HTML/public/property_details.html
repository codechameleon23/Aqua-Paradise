INTRODUCTION

Conveniently positioned within the popular village of West Wellow, enjoying excellent commuter links, this stunning home enjoys a private three acre site comprising a picturesque landscaped lake, exceptional entertainment terrace and delightful lawns backing onto private woodland. The property is constructed to the highest specification to include a Ugot integrated audio and visual system, luxurious fittings including Porcelenosa bathrooms and a stunning âCharles Rennie Mackintoshâ kitchen. The room dimensions are of excellent proportions, many of which feature doors opening to the delightful rear outlook. The impressive accommodation is complemented by four car garaging.

DESCRIPTION

The proposed accommodation is stunning, centred around a striking âCharles Rennie Mackintoshâ designed kitchen living room with full width bifold doors to the garden, supplemented by a substantial utility and boot room. The Mackintosh name has a heritage of meticulous craftsmanship and excellent design and comprises an extensive range of handleless, soft close, eye and base level units with âaurora fantasy graniteâ worktops. There is a host of fully integrated appliances including steam, combi and regular ovens with warming drawer. Other appliances include a wine cooler, dishwasher, induction hob and extractor, tall fridge, freezer and water softener. The central island provides contrasting units with a breakfast bar area and additional storage.

The spacious sitting room features bifold doors overlooking the lake. The cinema room is equipped with a projector, screen and black out blinds, integrated with the smart audio and visual system throughout the house with five in-ceiling speakers, a Blu-ray player and sub-woofer to enjoy the cinema experience. The dining room is triple aspect with bifold doors to the garden and a central woodburning stove inset to the feature fireplace. There is an additional reception room which can be used as a gym or family room and a contemporary shower room in addition to a separate downstairs WC.

An elegant glass and steel staircase ascends to the first floor with a galleried landing providing views across the New Forest National Park via a gable end window. There are five well proportioned bedrooms served by four luxurious bathrooms equipped with Porcelanosa tiling and sanitary ware. The master and guest suites also feature well sized dressing rooms. The house has structured cabling for distributing high definition video, music and data network from a central point to the majority of rooms. This also includes an integrated lighting system control.

OUTSIDE

A four car bay garage with automated doors provides ample car storage and there is additional storage area above. The property is approached via a long driveway culminating in a large turning space and parking for several vehicles. To the front of the garden is a picturesque lake surrounded by light woodland. To the rear of the property are formal gardens and paddocks whilst adjacent to the house is an impressive patio and entertainment terrace.
