Your Personal Stylist

Based in Belfast

At The Style Within You, you will be treated with the upmost attention, with my promise of discretion, professionalism and unrivalled customer service during and after our style sessions.

My aim as your Personal Stylist is to empower you with the confidence to know you look great, arming you with the tools to shop with purpose and success going forward. If time is the issue, I’m here to do it all for you!

We all have the ability to look incredible and to feel confident in our appearance. Together we will discover your WOW colours, and with my expert help you will understand your body shape and which fabrics and styles best complement. Lifestyle and personality play a huge part in everyone’s style discovery. Finding your own personal style will lead to a transformation in your confidence that will have such a positive impact that can be applied to both your professional and personal life. My styling advice will always be practical and tailored specifically to you, your personality and your lifestyle.

As a qualified Personal Stylist, I offer a range of services for both men and women including Colour & Style Discovery Consultations, Corporate Styling, Special Occasion Styling, Wardrobe Edits and Personal Shopping. I also offer Group Style Sessions and Bespoke Packages which can include a number of services perfectly tailored to your needs.

About Me

My professional career started in the corporate world of Events and Marketing, surrounded by clients and colleagues of all ages, shapes and sizes. I’ve always taken a huge interest in the style of others and how they have put their outfits together.

In 2016 just before going on maternity leave, I followed my passion for fashion by launching my own styling business with Global accessories and lifestyle brand, Stella & Dot. I loved my time with the brand helping hundreds of women find their own unique style and at the end of 2018 expanded this business by achieving a diploma in Personal Styling from the prestigious London College of Style. Since then, I have also achieved my advanced colour training with renowned Author and Colour Counsellor, Jules Standish.

As a working Mum, and someone who has experienced a fast-paced job and city life, I understand how busy and hectic life can be and the challenges you can face juggling the demands of work and life. ‘Me time’ doesn’t often come top of the list, in fact it usually falls to the bottom. We also go through periods of change in our bodies and lifestyle which can have a massive impact on energy levels and the way we feel about ourselves.

Whatever the reason, there is no better time to investment time in YOU. I am here to help create the best version of you by finding out who you really are; so together discovering The Style Within You.

Emma Phillips
