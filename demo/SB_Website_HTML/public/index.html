Our Priorities

To ensure the BID provides additional services to the city, we’ve consulted with city stakeholders to understand baseline agreements and what is already being provided as a statutory service. After analysing our consultation survey responses from businesses, we’ve identified three themes which summarise your priorities for the BID:
