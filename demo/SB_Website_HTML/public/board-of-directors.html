Meet the Board of Directors

The management and governance of the BID will be open and transparent and will be accountable to all levy payers. Salisbury BID operates as a company limited by guarantee and is governed by a voluntary Board of Directors who represent business members. The Board takes responsibility for the strategic and financial management of the BID. It is the responsibility of the Board of Directors to ensure good management of the BID. The Board will meet on a quarterly basis as a minimum. All BID members are eligible to apply to join the Board.

The BID team operates the day-to-day running of the BID and deliver the projects, services and initiatives set out in the BID’s business plan, as well as providing the Board with new initiatives and recommendations.
