My Philosophy

I was inspired to study nutrition when I was midway through a degree in drawing (bit of an about turn!). A student on my course asked me “What would you spend a £100 book voucher on, what section do you head to first?” My response was not design or art books but simply “The healthy cookery books, I love learning about foods and what they can do for us”.

My own health issues also influenced my decision to study nutrition. I had cripplingly bad IBS from my early 20’s, had recently been diagnosed with PCOS and found my weight had shot up and wasn’t coming off, and I had a decade long battle with acne and anxiety. I knew there had to be a better way of resolving these issues but I didn’t know how.
