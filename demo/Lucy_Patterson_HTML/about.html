About Lucy

I get it; it’s hard to eat well all the time. I’m all about a little flexibility when we can afford to. Sometimes taking a step into the unknown and challenging the beliefs you have (or the media have ingrained on you) can be the best action.

My Philosophy

I was inspired to study nutrition when I was midway through a degree in drawing (bit of an about turn!). A student on my course asked me “What would you spend a £100 book voucher on, what section do you head to first?” My response was not design or art books but simply “The healthy cookery books, I love learning about foods and what they can do for us”.

My own health issues also influenced my decision to study nutrition. I had cripplingly bad IBS from my early 20’s, had recently been diagnosed with PCOS and found my weight had shot up and wasn’t coming off, and I had a decade long battle with acne and anxiety. I knew there had to be a better way of resolving these issues but I didn’t know how.

Biography

I am a registered Nutritional Therapist (NT) with 5 years clinical experience. I have been trained in Functional Medicine principles towards supporting health and am always looking to determine the root imbalance and easiest way to correct this with my clients. I have worked with hundreds of clients over several years on a variety of health conditions, and my training and experience – combined with my compassion and realistic view of health – makes me a well-prepared partner for you in your journey towards better physical and mental health.

I graduated from the Institute for Optimum Nutrition in 2012 and have been in practice ever since. I am currently studying for my Masters degree in Advanced Nutrition Research and Practice, with a focus on individual carbohydrate tolerance and the gut micro biome.

I was lead nutrition practitioner for Foresight Preconception for 4 years, giving me valuable experience in preconception and fertility support as well as the barriers both men and women can face in their quest for optimal health and conception. I still offer Hair Mineral Analysis tests for clients wishing to explore their health and fertility using Foresight’s optimised ranges.

I had the pleasure of working for a HNWI VIP client in the Middle East for a year as a personal nutritional therapist, travelling the world as we worked together.

I have worked for Bodhimaya, international luxury retreats as their functional food expert and chef on several retreats and adore getting creative in the kitchen.
