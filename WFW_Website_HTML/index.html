Exclusive carp fishing in the UK

Welcome to Walden Estate Fishery, we are family run fishery in South Wiltshire. Walden offers exclusive fishing in picturesque and peaceful surroundings.

The fishery has been created to provide a challenging mixture of specimen carp fishing. The lakes have a great mix of beautiful mirror and common carp from a variety of different strains. Facilities include, lodge with toilet ,shower, bait freezer and electric points, secure parking. The fishery is located in West Grimstead 1 mile off the A36 and 5 miles from Salisbury.
