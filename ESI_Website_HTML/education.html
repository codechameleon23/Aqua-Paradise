Compared with other types of storage

Self Storage

Advantages

  • Very accessible if you need regular access

Disadvantages

  • Could be located on different floors
  • Long walks to the storage units
  • Narrow corridors to navigate, especially when others using facilities
  • Expensive storage solution
  • Due to the number of visitors, fire protection limited
  • Short notice price increases

Barn Storage

Advantages

  • Cheap

Disadvantages

  • Risk of fire
  • Low levels of security
  • Prone to fluctuations in temperatures
  • Not purpose built for storage business or home storage
  • It’s a barn!

Shipping containers

Advantages

  • Accessible within opening times

Disadvantages

  • Not purpose built for storage
  • The containers are ‘single skinned’
  • Prone to fluctuations in temperatures
  • Sweat inside the containers, causing condensation on storage items
