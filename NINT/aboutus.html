What We do?


SEMINAR ON BASIC WELDING & NDT :

Three days seminar imparts basic knowledge of non-destructive testing, types of NDT, purpose of NDT, advantages, limitations & applications of various NDT methods. This also includes familiarization of ASNT –SNT-TC-1A & ANSI/ASNT-CP-189

ASNT LEVEL III CONSULTANCY IN RT, UT, MT & PT

Preparing Written Practice for training, qualification & certification of NDT personnel suitable to company in line with ASNT- SNT – TC – 1A & ANSI/ASNT CP – 189 and NDT procedures as per reference codes, records of training, qualification & certification of NDT personnel’s.

CONSULTANCY FOR PREPARING INDUSTRIES FOR ISO 9000 CERTIFICATION

For the successful accomplishment of ISO 9000 certification, we provide guidance in designing and implementation of Quality Policy, Quality System Manual (QSM), Quality System Procedures (QSP), Internal Audit Systems and to promote the Quality culture at different levels of organization.

WELDING CONSULTANCY

Guidance for developing welding procedure specification (WPS), procedure qualification records, (PQR), Welders / Welding Operators Performance Qualification (WPQ) to meet the various international codes, standards and specifications. MTIS is your partner for Welding Solutions during manufacturing, installation and maintenance of various equipments at varied nature of projects.

WE ARRANGE HIGH SKILLED TECHNICAL MANPOWER FOR SHORT TERM & LONG TERM PROJECTS

We can mobilize ASNT NDT Level II and III personnel’s, AWS – CWI (Certified Welding Inspectors),Welding engineers, Project managers, Construction engineers, Site supervisors etc. to meet the various industrial requirements for short term and long term projects.
